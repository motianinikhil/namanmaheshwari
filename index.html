About Me

I am currently working as a Digital Design Engineer in the Automotive Radar Team at Texas Instruments, Bangalore, India.

My work mainly focusses on Built-In Self-Test (BIST) for memories in the memory-intensive Automotive Radar chip. Alongside, I am also working on improving the diagnostics in Multiple Input Signature Register (MISR) based compression architectures and Automatic Test Pattern Generation (ATPG) for advanced fault models like Small Delay Defect (SDD) and Path Delay.

I completed my bachelor’s degree in electrical and electronics from BITS-Pilani, Pilani in 2015 and had the opportunity to work on Approximate Multiplier Circuits during my internship at the University of Alberta, Canada.
